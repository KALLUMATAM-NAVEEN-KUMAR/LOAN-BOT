Bank chatbot

Chatbot - LoanBot is designed to answer customer questions about banks and their products, including services such as opening bank accounts, loans and other digital products. The chatbot is trained using customized data and then fed into deep learning models using the pytorch framework. Natural Language Processing (NLP) is used to manage, tokenize and lemmatize a group of data (sentences) in a .json archive. Chatbot - LoanBot is then trained using private data and can respond to almost any service-related information asked by the bank's customers.

Today's customers have busy schedules, so they don't have time to wait to contact a service representative. The most important thing is to ensure an immediate response to customers. LoanBot provides instant response centers to provide instant solutions and resolve your issues. LoanBot integrates with technologies such as NLP to extend semantic analysis and provide people with more interactive interactions.

In general, we can say that customers appreciate chatbots because they are more powerful and effective at answering questions than human representatives. Chatbot - LoanBot is able to answer simple questions from customers about various account types or other banking products offered by Bank.
